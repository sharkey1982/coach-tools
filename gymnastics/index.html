RISE Progression Pathway

Discover
Levels 1–3
Foundational movement and motor skills. Crawling, rolling, jumping, balancing, basic object control. Entry point for all pupils.
Explore
Levels 1–4
Structured gymnastics skills. Rolls, balances, jumps, leaps, cartwheels, handstands, apparatus work and group sequences.
Excel
Levels 1–3
Advanced and performance skills. Walkovers, somersaults, flysprings, complex apparatus, competition-standard sequencing.
Core Skills
Floor and body skills — shapes, rolls, balances, jumps, turns, leaps, handstands and acrobatic sequences.
Apparatus
Bench, bar, beam, vault and small apparatus skills — including hoop, rope, ball, and ribbon work.
Working Together
Partner and group work — counterbalances, acrobatic bases, formation work, sequences and displays.
